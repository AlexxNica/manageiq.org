Discovery

ManageIQ connects to the APIs of your virtualization, container, network and storage management systems and continuously discovers your resources and maps their relationships.

Self-service

ManageIQ allows you to define bundles of resources and publish them in a service catalog, from where they can be ordered by end users. ManageIQ manages the full life cycle service of a service including policy, delegated operations, showback, and end of life.

Compliance

ManageIQ can scan the contents of your VMs, hosts and containers, without the help of an agent. Using this data you can define advanced compliance policies and take action.

Optimization

ManageIQ captures metrics on your VMs, containers, and more. It can find unused systems, make right-sizing recommendations, and do capacity planning.
